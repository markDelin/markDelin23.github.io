Clarendon College Vision and Mission

Vision

Clarendon College endeavors to remain a premiere center of learning by uplifting the lives of our people through the delivery of high-quality and inclusive education with compassion and integrity.

Mission

At Clarendon College, we believe that all students should experience high-quality, holistic, and inclusive education that will result in personal transformation informed by Filipino values. We remain committed to providing our learners with a safe and positive environment to reach their highest potential by developing them into leaders who are responsive to the needs of local, national, and global communities.
